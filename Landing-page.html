Diversified Radiology is a team of top-tiered radiologists based in Denver, CO who are dedicated to the utmost medical care by ensuing that each radiologist concentrates exclusively on their individual specialties for which they have pursued. Diversified Radiology achieves their goals in providing the highest level of patient care by delivering superb image interpretation and intervention packaged with excellent patient and referring physician services. The subspecialties that Diversified Radiology focuses on are the areas of Neuro, Body, Cardiac,Vascular, Pediatric, Breast, Nuclear/PET and Musculoskeletal Imaging.
